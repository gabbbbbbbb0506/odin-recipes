Pinoy Spaghetti

Description

Filipino spaghetti is a Filipino adaptation of Italian spaghetti with Bolognese sauce. It has a distinctively sweet sauce, usually made from tomato sauce sweetened with brown sugar and banana ketchup. It is typically topped with sliced hot dogs or smoked longganisa sausages, giniling, and grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 pc onion
  • 1/4 kg ground pork
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 3/4 cup banana catsup
  • 1/2 cup hot dog
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 kg spaghetti noodles
  • 3 tbsp grated cheese

Steps

  1. Saute garlic, onion and groud pork in oil. add tomato sauce, catsup and hotdog. Pour water andd simmer. Season with salt and pepper. Add brown sugar and set aside.
  2. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  3. Strain spaghetti and toss in the sauce. Transfer on a serving plate, top with cheese and serve.
